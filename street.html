Cross-Cutting Procedures

Strict adherence to food safety is mandatory across all Market Street and Cafe areas.

  • Follow correct hand washing procedures.
  • Maintain 'Clean As You Go' discipline.
  • Adhere to temperature control for storage, prep, display, and cooking.
  • Prevent cross-contamination (separate boards, utensils, cleaning).
  • Complete Legal Logbooks accurately and on time.

Refer to specific sections in each department's guide and the Safe & Legal Page for full details.

Accurate labelling is a legal requirement.

  • Ensure all Pre-Packed for Direct Sale (PPDS) items have full ingredients and allergens clearly listed (Natasha's Law).
  • Use correct PLUs on Bizerba scales/GLP printers. Refer to Street Lists.
  • Maintain Bizerba scales/GLP printers: Ensure they are online, clean, and labels aligned correctly.
  • Report labelling issues via Technology Helpdesk.

Refer to specific sections in each department's guide and the Safe & Legal Page for full details.

Specific markdown times apply to Market Street products:

  • General Markdown Window: Mon - Sat 5pm, Sun 3pm (4pm Scotland) - Unless exceptions apply.
  • Bakery Exceptions: Baguettes/Sticks (Waste EOD), Doughnuts (Waste EOD / TGTG), Rolls (Reduced bag up 7:30pm, sell next day), Crusty Rolls (Waste EOD).
  • Deli Exceptions: Cooked Meats (50% markdown EOD counter life, move to clearance).
  • Hot Food Exceptions: Oven Fresh/Pies (50% markdown 1hr before close).
  • Salad Bar Exceptions: Pot up remaining at 6pm.
  • Cafe Pre-Packed: Sandwiches (50% markdown 5pm EOD).
  • Loose Produce: Follow selected 2nd markdown process. Berries only one markdown 5pm EOD.
  • Plants: Markdown based on condition/shelf life.

Process all waste accurately using HHTs. Participate in daily Waste Parade.
