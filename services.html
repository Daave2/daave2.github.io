Front End Services Overview

The Front End (Checkouts, CSD, Kiosk, SCO, Car Park) is crucial for efficient, friendly service. Key principles include Customer First behaviours, queue management (1+1 rule), clean service areas, and operational equipment.

Access the full Front End Operating Guidance here.

Service Behaviours & Standards

Consistently demonstrate:

  1. Greet Customers: Friendly acknowledgement.
  2. Offer a Basket: Proactive assistance.
  3. Answer Questions: Helpful attitude, aim for "Yes".

Refer to the Customer First 15-point plan on the Operations page for full details.

Ref: Front End Op Guide p3

Standard: 1 customer served + 1 customer waiting (1+1).

  • Main Bank: Min. 1 till open always. Schedule based on planners/demand.
  • Self Scan (SCO): Manned during store hours. Colleague wears hi-vis, assists proactively, manages cash/card modes, replenishes consumables, focuses on Service & Protect.
  • Kiosk/CSD: Manned during specified hours. Use headset for support.
  • Monitoring: Service Team Leaders visually monitor, use headset/baton, check Efficiency Measure Dashboard (Looker).

Operational Area Guides

Layout & Standards:

  • Clean, tidy, clutter-free desks/kiosks.
  • Correct signage displayed (Refunds, Licensing, C25, etc.).
  • Essential equipment available (Lottery terminal, Vocovo, Till rolls).
  • Lost property managed via Salesforce (secure storage, 4-week retention).
  • Kiosk Bunker organised, stock rotated.

Key Tasks:

  • Refunds/Exchanges (See dedicated accordion).
  • Lottery/Scratchcards.
  • Lost & Found logging/reclaiming (Salesforce).
  • Mart Cart checks/booking (Salesforce).
  • Visitor sign-in & fire safety briefing.
  • Handling Cash Office items (Saver Stamps, Gift Vouchers - See Cash Office section).
  • Accepting partner delivery driver collections (e.g. Deliveroo).

Ref: Front End Op Guide p5, 15

Checkout WGLL:

  • Cleanliness: Belts, bagging areas, drawers, floors.
  • Equipment: Tags stored, bins empty, consumables replenished, Vocovo ready.
  • Merchandising: Queue lines correct, impulse tidy.
  • Service: Helpful, friendly, efficient, Key Behaviours met.

SCO WGLL:

  • Signage: Correct Cash/Card, Contactless, C25 visible.
  • Cleanliness: Surfaces, scanner glass clean.
  • Equipment: Bags/rolls replenished, headset ready, detagger secure, tag bin available/emptied, SCO cabinet stocked.
  • Colleague Presence: Hi-vis worn, proactive assist, vigilant (Service & Protect).
  • More Card Scanning: Ensure customers scan More Card for points/offers.
  • Catalina Coupons: Understand process, troubleshoot basic printer issues (See More Card Guide p38-39).

Ref: Front End Op Guide p10-14

Trolley Standards:

  • Returned regularly to bays, clean, undamaged. Secured at close.
  • Bays clean, posters correct. Report damage to Wanzl/Maintenance.
  • Order new/report damaged trolleys via form (link in guide). Wanzl partnership manages maintenance (SLAs apply).
  • Report theft >9 trolleys to LP Control Room (0345 611 6711), <9 via MySecurity.
  • Use Trolleywise app/email (trolleywise@wanzl.co.uk) for abandoned trolleys.

Basket Standards:

  • Stacked <50 high. Located correctly (in-store, not blocking exits).
  • All types available, not stored outside.

General Area WGLL:

  • Clean, litter-free, recycling areas clear, external signage good.
  • Safety: Grit available (winter), no obstructions, good lighting.

Ref: Front End Op Guide p16 & Trolley/Basket Guide

Refund & Exchange Policy:

  • 30-Day Change of Mind: Requires proof of purchase, original re-saleable condition. Exclusions apply (check policy).
  • Faulty Goods: Statutory rights apply; always returnable.
  • Process: Process at CSD/Kiosk using correct Till Refund Codes. >£11 requires manager check slip.

Goodwill Guidelines:

  • Listen, apologise, resolve first time.
  • Use Goodwill Matrix (£5-£10 typical value via product/gift card). Store Manager auth needed for >£10 (max £30 on refund card).
  • Use 'Goodwill' tender for product goodwill.
  • Log complex complaints/Foreign Body/Illness via Salesforce.

More Card Support

Front End colleagues are key to supporting customers with the More Card scheme.

View Full More Card Operating Guide (PDF)

  • Offer sign-up via App, Website, or Salesforce (in-store). See Guide p6, 29.
  • Temporary cards available at CSD while physical card is delivered (up to 14 days).
  • Report Lost/Stolen via App, Website, or Salesforce (Guide p30). New digital card immediate, physical within 14 days.
  • Explain points earning (Fuel, specific products). Balance visible on app, website, till receipts (Guide p18).
  • Explain Fiver conversion (5000 points = £5). Digital fivers via app/website, physical print automatically for non-digital users (Guide p12-14).
  • Troubleshooting common issues (app won't load, fiver not applying, passcode not received) - see Guide p26. Log Salesforce case if unresolved.
  • Adding missed points/collector spend from receipts (Guide p32-33).
  • Updating voucher preferences (digital vs print) via Salesforce (Guide p31).
  • Explain More Card Prices (exclusive discounts scanned at till). Identify via SELs/App (Guide p19).
  • Explain More Rewards (personalised app offers needing activation) (Guide p20).
  • Explain Collectors (e.g., Christmas Collector - track spend via app/coupon) (Guide p21).
  • Explain Points Boosters (app-only offers on specific brands/categories) (Guide p22).

Cash Office Procedures (Front End Interaction)

Front End colleagues interact with Cash Office processes daily.

View Full Cash Office Operating Guide (PDF)

  • **Loans:** Receive change loans from Cash Office/Manager at start of day/shift. Process via Till Function > Till Loan (Guide p11).
  • **Lifts (ICH Pouches):** Securely handle cash removed from tills/SCOs via ICH pouches. Correct process for entering value as Till Lift on Storeline crucial for balancing (Guide p28).
  • **Balancing/Over Shorts:** Understand Till Over/Short report. Cooperate with investigations for discrepancies >£10 (Guide p36-40).
  • **Till Locking:** Ensure tills are correctly actioned for Till Locking process by Cash Office (usually 8am) (Guide p31).
  • **Manual Tenders:** Handle specific tenders correctly (e.g., manual EFT fallbacks, specific vouchers - requires clear understanding of what's accepted).
  • **Lottery/Scratchcards:** Correctly reconcile sales/prizes at Kiosk/till against Lottery terminal reports (Guide p17-22).
  • **Banking Prep:** Ensure coupons, vouchers, specific tenders are correctly separated and processed for Cash Office banking prep (Guide p54).

Pharmacy Interaction (Reference Only)

The Pharmacy operates separately, but Front End staff may interact with patients/customers.

View Full Pharmacy Operating Guide (PDF)

  • Direct all prescription and specific medicine queries directly to the Pharmacy counter/Pharmacist.
  • Be aware of Pharmacy opening hours (these may differ from main store).
  • Locate the Pharmacy within the store to provide directions.
  • Understand that P-Medicines can only be sold when a Pharmacist is present.
  • Familiarise yourself with basic services offered (e.g., Flu Jabs - seasonal) to signpost customers, but always refer them to Pharmacy staff for details.
