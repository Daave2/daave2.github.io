Emergency Procedures

Immediate Actions: Safety First > Establish Control > Assess Severity > Notify/Escalate.

Escalation

  • Minor (P4/5): Duty Manager.
  • Substantial (P3): Regional Mgr/People Mgr.
  • Severe (P1/2): Ops Director/Head of People.

Key Contacts

  • Hilmore: 0345 611 6711
  • LP Control: Opt 1 > Opt 2
  • Press Office: 0345 877 8778

Replenishment

  • Models: Twilight / Night Fill / Drop & Fill (12pm+).
  • Tools: Inbound Dashboard, Microlise Tracker.
  • Priorities: High traffic (Milk, Bread, Produce) > Promo > Backstock.
  • Process: Pre-sort > Fill > Capping Shelf Principles > Rumble (4pm).
  • Standards: One Product One Place, remove cardboard, face up.
