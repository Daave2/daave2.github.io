Safe and Legal Compliance

Maintain a safe, compliant environment by adhering to procedures. "Safety First" ensures we protect our colleagues and customers.

Licensing & Age

  • Display: Premises Licence & Auth to Sell Alcohol must be visible.
  • Authorisation: Notices updated with current staff.
  • Challenge 25: Mandatory for alcohol/restricted items. No valid ID = No Sale.
  • Signage: Displayed at all sales points.
  • Refusals: Record all challenges/refusals accurately.

Audits & Checks

  • Logbooks: Complete Front End, Cafe, Counters, Cleaning logs daily.
  • Internal Audits: Action Cash Loss/Safe & Legal audit points promptly.
  • Self Audits: Use Dymension for regular compliance checks.
  • Daily Checks: Fridge temps, Fire exits, Alarms, Chip & Pin.
  • Natasha's Law: Daily PPDS labelling checks (by 2pm).

Health & Safety

  • Warehouse: Clear exits, safe MHE use (trained only), PPE.
  • Manual Handling: Assess risks, use aids.
  • Hazards: Clean spills ('Wet Floor' signs), clear trip hazards.
  • Fire: Clear routes, know assembly points, check extinguishers.
  • Equipment: Use safely (guards in place), Lockout/Tagout for maintenance.
  • COSHH: Store chemicals safely, use PPE.
  • Accidents: Report via MySecurity.

Food Safety

  • HACCP/Temps: Monitor fridge/freezer temps daily. Cold chain (20min rule).
  • Dates/Rotation: FIFO. Strict Use By adherence. Accurate date coding.
  • Hygiene: 'Clean As You Go'. Personal hygiene (uniform/handwashing).
  • Allergens: Prevent cross-contamination. Accurate info (Natasha's Law).
  • Pest Control: clean environment, reporting sightings, bin management.
  • Waste: Segregate correctly. Secure bins.

Data & Security

  • GDPR: Handle personal data securely.
  • Security: Secure cash/keys. Report suspicious activity.
  • Logins: No sharing. Lock unattended screens.
  • CCTV: Awareness and correct request procedure.
  • Whistleblowing: 0800 084 3474 for serious concerns.
